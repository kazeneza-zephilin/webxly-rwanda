About Us

Wexly Rwanda is a development-focused studio helping individuals, startups, and small businesses bring their digital ideas to life. We believe great tech shouldn't be expensive or complicated — it should simply work.

We operate with a flexible, hybrid model. Our core team handles strategy, planning, and development direction, while we collaborate with trusted freelancers when projects grow bigger. This lets us deliver fast, maintain high quality, and keep our services affordable.

Whether you need a simple website or a fully custom application, we're here to make sure your digital journey feels smooth, friendly, and stress-free.
